    • Kevin Healy

    Research Fellow in Zoology at Trinity College Dublin - IE

    PhD Student
    healyke@tcd.ie
     @healyke

    I am mainly interested in using analytical and comparative approaches in order to understand interactions between individuals and species across a wide ranges of scale. My current research centres on the influence of body size and environmental complexity on traits associated with predator-prey interactions such as life-history traits, information processing abilities, scavenging ability and venom production.

    I also actively communicate my work through teaching and by promoting evolutionary sciences to the general public (namely through the EcoEvo@TCD blog and events such as Discover Research Night.
